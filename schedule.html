Società Italiana di Biochimica e Biologia Molecolare (SIB)

Characterizing molecular interactions and biological networks to understand the complexity of living systems

Declaratory Group

"Computational and Systems Biology"

Starting from structural and functional information on nucleic acids, proteins, and their variants, we aim to characterize the molecular interactions and networks underlying the biological processes determining the biological complexity of living beings.

We aim to promote experimental and computational research and training in this sector for a better understanding of biological and biotechnological processes to support personalized medicine for pathologies, both multifactorial and genetic.
